ABOUT

DJ and producer Mahmut Orhan was born on January 11, 1993, in the city of Bursa, Turkey. He primarily works in the styles of deep house, indie house, and nu-disco.
The talented musician began his career at the age of 15. Later, he moved to Istanbul, where he worked as a DJ in some of the largest nightclubs. In 2012, his track "Ceiron" made it to the "Little Buddha 5" series, and "Fringe" to "Siddharta Dubai." Success awaited him after these releases.
In 2014, Mahmut presented the track "Without You Ep" on the Beachside Records label. He also released an album called "Compilation" in collaboration with the Chilai club, where Orhan worked as a music director. The DJ participated in many parties in Bursa, Antalya, Istanbul, and Izmir.
The musician's first real hit was the track "Age of Emotions," which was released in 2015. Another notable composition, "Feel" (featuring Sena Sener), gained international popularity. In 2016, it entered the charts in several countries, including Poland, Bulgaria, Greece, Kazakhstan, Romania, and Ukraine.
In 2017, he released the song "Save me," recorded in collaboration with the singer Eneli.
