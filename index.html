Mahmut Orhan is a Turkish producer and DJ who started his music career while still a child. He gained popularity in 2015 thanks to the song “Age of Emotions,” and just a year later, he released the global hit “Feel,” which topped music charts across Europe and took the #1 spot on iTunes.


In 2017, he remixed the “Game of Thrones” opening theme before releasing the song “Save Me.” Both achieved great success. A year later, he released a remix album titled “One,” while his single “6 Days” became famous worldwide, topping charts in many countries. Since then, this Turkish DJ has been among the leading festivals’ most sought-after guests.
